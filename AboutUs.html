About Us

    My name is Jenna Lynn Ariza and I am the owner of this website. At the moment, I am a first-year University Student at New Jersey Institute of Technology (NJIT). I am studying as an Human Computer Interaction (HCI) major, who is thinking of minoring in something like Graphic Design so that I can become UX Designer in the near future. Asides from my studies, I work at my part-time job at Panera Bread in Hoboken, New Jersey. I also enjoy painting, hanging out with my friends, eating food, playing video games, and spending time with my hamster (of course)!

    Cleo is one of my first real pets. She is a short-haired Syrian hamster. I purchased her from PetSmart in Brooklyn, New York. It was kind of a spontaneous buy; I usually just stroll around New York with my friends and family and one day I saw her cuddled up in her hamster home. I decided to buy her because (1) not only was she cute, but (2) her window said "Rainy day, I'll be your Sunshine!" and was instantly super duper sweet when I held her. She's never bitten me and is super loving and friendly with everyone she meets. I am unsure as to how old she is today but it's almost been a year since she came into my life, but I am sure I wouldn't trade her for anything in the world.
