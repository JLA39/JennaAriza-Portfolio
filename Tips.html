Cages for Hamsters

1st Time Suggestions

    My name is Jenna Lynn Ariza and I am the owner of this website. When I first got Cleo, it was from Petsmart. Although, it wasn't ideally the best place to adopt from, I still made some mistakes in purchasing her new home for her. In my eyes, alongside many others, I believe that PetSmart makes inhumane cages for hamsters. If you are planning on buying a cage or two and supplies for your new hamster(s), make sure it isn't entirely plastic and bars - it's a prison for them. For my Cleo, she sure didn't like it from day one.

Taking Your Hamster Outside

My Opinion

    If you'd like to take your hamster outside, you should always be careful. Make sure you never leave them unsupervised to themselves. It would be best if you are with them at all times to unsure they won't get hurt or lost. Some possible places to go to would be a grassy area, such as your backyard! For me, I don't have a backyard so I take her to the park with her in-place wheel and let her walk around and eat her snacks on a picnic blanket. She certainly loves it!
